Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 pcs, medium, -- cucumber
  • 2 pcs, ripe, medium -- tomatoes
  • 1 tsp, olive oil for dressing
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cut cucumber in two halves and, then, slice it into thin slices
  2. Cut the tomato in halves and remove the spot where the tomato stalk has been attached
  3. Cut the tomato into thin slices, toss with salt and wait for the juices to get separated
  4. Toss with olive oil and, then, serve cold. Bon appetit!
